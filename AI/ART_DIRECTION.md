# Art Direction

## Core Theme
**"Zundamon's Kitchen"** is set in a vibrant, whimsical world where nature and culinary arts coexist. The environment is lush, filled with "Zunda" (green pea) inspired flora.

## Characters
- **Zundamon (🍙)**: The primary guide and kitchen master. Calm, wise, and slightly sleepy.
- **Zundapal (🍡)**: The energetic assistant. Enthusiastic, loves Zunda mochi, and provides frequent encouragement.
- **Tone**: Always supportive and friendly. Dialogue uses "~" to indicate a gentle, singing tone. Emojis are used to convey emotion.

## Environment Design
- **Gathering Nodes**: Should look like oversized, stylized plants (flowers, berries, pea pods).
- **Atmosphere**: Use dynamic sky systems (see `DayNightSky.server.lua`) to create cozy golden hours and starry nights.
- **Color Consistency**: Environment colors should lean towards the Primary/Secondary greens and Pink/Lavender accents.

## Aesthetic Goals
1. **Cozy**: Players should feel safe and relaxed.
2. **Whimsical**: Magic is part of the world (e.g., floating accents, glowing ingredients).
3. **Kawaii**: Characters and UI should be cute and expressive.
